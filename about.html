>_ About Me

I'm Cristian Muscai, a passionate developer and electronics enthusiast from Romania. With a strong foundation in Visual Programming, I specialize in creating interactive educational tools and simulations that bridge the gap between theory and practice.

My work often explores the intersection of physics, numerical methods, and user-friendly interfaces, aiming to make complex concepts accessible and engaging. Whether it's simulating electric field lines or developing intuitive GUIs, I strive to combine technical rigor with creative design.
